CEO Biography

Chhai was born and raised from Long Beach California.

The child of two refugee parents, money was often scarce.

As a way to escape the dangers of street violence, Chhai enlisted into the military after high school.

The military allowed him to find his passion with vehicles and even provided him with mechanical skills.
